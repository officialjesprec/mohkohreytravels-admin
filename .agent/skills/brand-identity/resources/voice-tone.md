# Voice & Tone Guidelines

- **Professional**: Communication needs to reflect the premium, high-trust nature of international travel and visa processing. No slang or overly casual language.
- **Direct**: Avoid unnecessary fluff or ambiguity. Clear "What's Included/Not Included", transparent pricing (Dynamic NGN/USD toggle), and concise calls-to-action outperforming long paragraphs.
- **Empathetic**: Understand the stress involved in cross-border logistics (especially visa and study abroad applications). The phrasing should inspire confidence, like "We handle the paperwork, you start packing."
